Welcome to the Wisconsin Association of College Stores (WACS) Website. WACS is a professional, non-profit association that welcomes both college stores and vendors that serve the Universities, Colleges, Community Colleges and Technical Colleges of the state of Wisconsin.

The purpose of our Association is to promote the professional development of our members through support, networking, educational opportunities and annual meetings.

We would like to invite all our members and visitors to have a look around. If you have any questions or comments about the site or the Association, please do not hesitate to contact me at wacs.wi@gmail.com and I, or one of our Board Members will reply.
